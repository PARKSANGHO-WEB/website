    AMPOULE line

    Concentrated serums that quickly
    boost nutrient levels & elasticity

    Size : 885mm(W) x 865mm(D) x 1350mm(H)
    Weight : 85Kg (Full package)
    Laser diode power : 60W Max

    Line Introduction

    Ampoule Line Overview

    - Concentrated serums designed to quickly boost moisture and nutrient
    levels and to increase the skin’s elasticity from deep within.
    - Provides complete hydration
    - Bolsters the skin’s natural defenses
    - Guards against moisture loss for 12 to 36 hours

    How To Use

    Apply evenly over the entire face after cleansing and toning
