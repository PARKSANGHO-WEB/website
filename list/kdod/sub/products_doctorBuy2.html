    Individual Items

    Micellar Gentle Lip & Eye Remover (200ml)

    Special Features

    - [Gentle lip and eye makeup remover] Efficiently remove makeup residue from sensitive skin around the eyes and lips by plant extracts - [Skin-protectant and soothing properties] Help sooth irritated skin around the eyes and lips immediately upon application by centella asiatica extract, ficus carica fruit extract & ulmus davidiana root extract - [Highly moisturizing ingredients to prevent dryness] Help moisturizing the skin and improve its immunity by amaranthus caudatus seed extract, dipotassium glycyrrhizate & allatoin

    Active Ingredients

    - [Ficus Carica (Fig) Fruit Extract] Proven effects on skin melanin, transepidermal loss, hydration values, and sebum content. - [Ulmus Davidiana Root Extract] A medicinal herb. It cares the skin as clean & healthy with antioxidant effect, soothes irritated skin and improves skin tone. - [Ceramide NP] Increases moisture, supports renewal of the skin’s natural protective layer and forms an effective barrier against moisture loss glycyrrhizate & allatoin

    How To Use

    1. Shake well before using. 2. After saturating, an appropriate amount of solution to a cotton pad 3. apply the cotton pad onto eye/lip areas and hold for several seconds 4. Then wipe in one direction to remove make-up residue
