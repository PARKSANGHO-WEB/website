    AFTER TREATMENT (for Patients)

    1. Gently massage the area twice a day for 5-10 minutes (up to 12 weeks)
    2. Drink enough water and do light physical activities
    3. Treated skin can be more sun-sensitive for a while. Apply SPF 30 or higher sunscreen.
    4. Mild redness, tenderness, swelling or tissue firmness/nodules may be present after treatment and they usually be disappered
    without medical care
    5. Tenderness may last up to 2 weeks or longer in some cases. If needed, use a cold compress and/or acetaminophen (Tylenol)
    to help relieve tenderness
    6. You can restart normal daily activity including exercise immediately after treatment
    7. Contact your doctor if you have any concerns about your treatment areas
