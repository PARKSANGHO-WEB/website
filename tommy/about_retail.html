FRANCHISE STORES

Franchise stores are run by partners with consolidated experience in the fashion industry and retail know-how, combined with a strong knowledge of local markets. The Tommy Hilfiger franchise partnership merges global brand management with local retail excellence.

COMPLETE PACKAGE

We offer the franchise system throughout Europe, the Middle East and Africa and we provide a complete package that covers all aspects of franchise management, including fully developed and detailed store concepts designed by Tommy Hilfiger, store project management, buying support, retail marketing, visual merchandising, learning and development, performance management and store operations support.
